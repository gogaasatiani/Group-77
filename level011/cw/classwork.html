what is fasanauris khinkali

Pasanauri khinkali is a specific type of Georgian soup dumpling, originating from the mountain village of Pasanauri, which is famous for its flavorful and juicy dumplings. These "mountain dumplings" are often prepared with a meat filling (typically lamb, beef, or pork mixed with spices like cumin and chili), and the dough is designed to hold a savory, hot broth.
